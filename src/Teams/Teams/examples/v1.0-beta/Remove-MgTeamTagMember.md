### Example 1: Using the Remove-MgTeamTagMember Cmdlet
```powershell
Import-Module Microsoft.Graph.Teams
Remove-MgTeamTagMember -TeamId $teamId -TeamworkTagId $teamworkTagId -TeamworkTagMemberId $teamworkTagMemberId
```
