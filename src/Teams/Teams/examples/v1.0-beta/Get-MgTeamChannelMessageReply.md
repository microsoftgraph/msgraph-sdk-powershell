### Example 1: Using the Get-MgTeamChannelMessageReply Cmdlet

Import-Module Microsoft.Graph.Teams
Get-MgTeamChannelMessageReply -TeamId $teamId -ChannelId $channelId -ChatMessageId $chatMessageId
